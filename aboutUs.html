Samaj Kalyan Mandal is a non-governmental organization (NGO) is any non-profit, voluntary citizens' group which is organized on a local level in Bihar, India. It is established by RESHU PRATAP in 2019. The main aim of Samaj Kalyan Mandal is to empower underprivileged children especially girls through relevant education, innovative healthcare and market-focused livelihood programmes.
